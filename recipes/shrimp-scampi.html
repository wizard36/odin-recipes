Shrimp scampi recipe

Description

This is a simple recipe for shrimp scampi that includes shrimp, butter, garlic, white wine, lemon juice, and fresh parsley. The shrimp are saut√©ed in a skillet with the butter and garlic, and then the white wine and lemon juice are added to the skillet. The sauce is then reduced and the shrimp are served over pasta or rice.

Ingedients

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of white wine
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and cook for about 30 seconds, or until it's fragrant.
  2. Add the shrimp to the skillet and season them with salt and pepper. Cook the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they're pink and opaque.
  3. Pour the white wine and lemon juice over the shrimp and stir in the chopped parsley. Cook for an additional minute, or until the sauce has reduced slightly.
  4. Serve the shrimp scampi over pasta or rice, with a sprinkle of parsley on top. Enjoy!
